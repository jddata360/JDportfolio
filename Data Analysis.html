About Data

My objective here is to utilize most excel features which I had learned using LinkedIn learning. As a part of my job I do regular data analysis and visualization techniques, However, I felt this isnt enough to showcase my talent.Hence I utilized open source platform to collect the raw data to analyse and build a dashboard. This dashboard consists of few aspects based on the questions.

  • What is the data all about ?
  • How the data could be categorized ?
  • Within the categorization is there any dependency or relation between the data ?
  • What is the outcome ?

Results

Fast food Sales data analysis is interesting to work with.Two different sheet had information on order id and menu information, using these data as a unique identifiers tried to get the infoormation using Lookup functions in excel so the necessary information is gathered all in one sheet.Using tezt function analyzefor further analysis.Used IF function to check on the sales data if this is ordered multiple times.Finally, used pivot table to gather all the information as a form of table - utilized graphs to intrepret the data. Overall results for Q1 : Jan, Feb and March

  • Italian , Asian , Mexican, American
  • Overall Sales is $ 159,217.90
  • In these 3 months : Italian had the highest sales, followed by Asian, Mexican and American.
  • In particular, Sphagetti & Meatballs topping the chart with the Top 10 sold food variety in Italian cuisine.
  • During weekends : Sun the sales were high compared to other days, on the other hand weekdays during Lunch hours ( 12- 1pm) had a high sales.
  • Italian category provided the maximum sales, this has been evident on the weekend sales too
  • Sundays has a maximum of 299 orders.
  • Weekends and other holidays like New Year's eve has contributed good sales.
